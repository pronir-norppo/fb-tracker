How to enable name auto-fill in your Google Form

  1. In your Google Form, add fields: Name, Unit, Date
  2. Open the live form URL in Chrome → right-click a field → Inspect
  3. Find the name attribute, e.g. entry.123456789
  4. In this HTML file, find the ENTRY_NAME, ENTRY_UNIT, ENTRY_DATE lines and replace the placeholder IDs
  5. Google Sheets auto-captures all responses — no extra setup needed
